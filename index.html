About

A barber on Tower Street.

King’s Barbers is a small, independent shop on Tower Street — the medieval thread that runs between the Saturday Market and the Tuesday Market in the old part of King’s Lynn.

We do one thing: cut men’s hair, and tidy beards. No appointment system, no website to wrestle with, no app. You come in, you have a seat, you wait your turn, and you leave looking sharp.

The shop has been part of the Tower Street row for years alongside the other independents that make this corner of PE30 worth walking to. If the chair’s free, we’ll have you in it. If it isn’t, the wait is rarely long.

Services

What we do, plainly.

  1. i. Men’s Haircut

    Clipper, scissor, or a combination — whatever the haircut calls for. Sides tidied, neckline finished clean.

  2. ii. Beard Trim

    Shape, line up, and trim. Lengths kept consistent, edges taken in with a straight razor.

  3. iii. Hot Towel Shave

    The full ritual — warm towels, pre-shave oil, lather, straight razor, cold towel to finish. Forty-odd minutes in the chair.

  4. iv. Head Shave

    Clean, even, properly close. Finished with balm.
